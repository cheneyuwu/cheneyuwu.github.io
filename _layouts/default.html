Hello! I am an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto majoring in Engineering Science (Robotics) where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Florian Shkurti and Prof. Jonathan Kelly. I am deeply interested in machine learning and robotics, and my research focuses on developing learning algorithms for robot vision, planning, and control. More specifically, I am willing to develop methods that enable robots to learn a set of composable and generalizable skills, which can be used to carrying out complicated and possibly unseen tasks. I also want to find ways that allow robots to coordinate with each other easily and efficiently and interact with humans in a shared workspace safely and reliably.

Last summer, I worked with Prof. Florian Shkurti on combining imitation learning and reinforcement learning for robotic control. Before that, I was an software engineer intern at Intel PSG where I participated in the development of the Intel HLS Compiler for FPGA and Intel SDK for OpenCL Applications.

If you have any questions/want to talk/want to collaborate, feel free to send me an email!
